Homology Modeling

What is Homology Modeling?

Homology Modeling is a computational method used to predict the 3D structure of a protein based on its similarity to a known protein structure. Since protein structure determines function, this technique plays a critical role in structural bioinformatics, drug discovery, and molecular biology research.

Steps of Homology Modeling

1.Template Selection

Identify a known protein structure similar to the target sequence.

2.Sequence Alignment

Align the target protein sequence with the template.

3.Model Building

Construct the 3D structure using computational tools.

4.Model Validation

Evaluate the accuracy and quality of the predicted model.

Applications

  • Drug design and vaccine development
  • Understanding genetic mutations
  • Protein function predication
  • Disease research
